Mental Health

What is it?

"Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood."
Source: Mentalhealth.gov

The importance of mental health in our daily lives is vast
Mental health is directly related to a healthy lifestyle and therefore,
a happy life. Good mental health can help you in relationships, stress tackling and productivity.

Always look after your mental health because your happiness matters the most.

What to do?

    Taking care of one's mental health is very important. Here are some tips to maintain a good mental health:

  • Talk about your feelings
  • Take a break
  • Do what makes you happy
  • Remember you value in life
  • Spend time with your loved ones
  • Stay in good company
  • Do not hesitate in asking for help
