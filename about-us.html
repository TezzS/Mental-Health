ABOUT US

FYS has been built on the pillars of trust, care and kindness. We have only one mission and that is to spread smiles. Not only does FYS encourages you to share your expierences but also asks you to be receptive and kind to others. Branded in the late 80s, FYS soon became a beacon of hope for thousands of consumers.
